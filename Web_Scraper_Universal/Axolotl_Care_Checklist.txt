AXOLOTL CARE CHECKLIST
======================

Daily Tasks:
□ Check water temperature (60-64°F / 16-18°C)
□ Feed axolotl (bloodworms, pellets, or earthworms)
□ Remove uneaten food after 15 minutes
□ Check water level and top off if needed
□ Observe axolotl behavior and health

Weekly Tasks:
□ Perform 20% water change
□ Test water parameters (pH 6.5-8.0, ammonia 0 ppm, nitrite 0 ppm, nitrate <20 ppm)
□ Clean any visible algae from glass
□ Check filter operation
□ Inspect hide spots and decorations

Bi-Weekly Tasks:
□ Perform 30-40% water change
□ Clean aquarium decorations
□ Vacuum substrate (if using sand)
□ Rinse filter media in old tank water

Monthly Tasks:
□ Deep clean aquarium glass
□ Replace filter cartridges if needed
□ Inspect all equipment (heater, filter, air pump)
□ Check for signs of illness or stress
□ Trim any live plants

Important Notes:
- Never use soap or chemicals in the tank
- Always use dechlorinated water
- Keep tank in a cool, dark area
- No gravel substrate (sand or bare bottom only)
- Tank mates: None recommended (axolotls are solitary)
- Minimum tank size: 20 gallons for one axolotl
- Filtration: Sponge filter or low-flow canister filter
- Lighting: Minimal (axolotls prefer darkness)
